Product introduction

The drill rig EP2000E is an all-in-one system for soil and groundwater sampling, monitoringand site remediation with a compact platform. Applications include soil sampling, soil vapolsampling, in-situ direct push injection,rock coring, groundwater monitoring, geotechnicaland more. lt has sufficient power with low energy consumption, and has the functions otsystem fault diagnosis, satellite positioning and loT communication.
