Product introduction

EP5100A is a powerful multi-purpose drill rig geotechnical engineering and exploration.It can be applied to low-disturbance drilling and coring in complex strata such as soil, gravel layers, rock formations, groundwater stratification sampling, in-situ testing, and environmental remediation control, etc.

P5100A adopts acoustic drilling technology, using high-frequency vibration force, rotational force and static pressure. The three forces work together and utilize the local "liquefaction effect" of the formation to quickly cut the drill bit into the formation.It is a new, internationally advanced full-stratum low-disturbance drilling technology method.It can sample various complex strata, including sandy soil layers, silt layers, soft soil layers, hard soil layers, pebble layers, rock layers, etc.

  • Environmental drilling sampling and testing;
  • Geotechnical engineering sampling and testing;
  • Geotechnical engineering construction;
  • Environmental remediation and risk management;
  • Mineral survey drilling;
  • Hydrology and water conservancy drilling;
  • Seismic drilling sampling.
