Who We Are

DrillPush is a Brazilian company specialized in equipment rental, machinery and tool sales, and service delivery for the contaminated areas, geotechnics, and foundations markets.

Focused on complex industrial sites, large-scale projects, and initiatives within the context of Contaminated Area Management (CAM), the company also stands out in the execution of environmental and geotechnical surveys and high-resolution subsurface characterization services (HRSC – High Resolution Site Characterization).

Built by professionals with over 20 years of industry experience, DrillPush combines technical expertise, technology, agility, and reliability to deliver efficient, safe, and project-specific solutions.

As an Eprobe System partner, DrillPush expands its portfolio with advanced environmental investigation and drilling technologies, bringing internationally proven solutions to the Brazilian market and delivering gains in data quality, reduced technical uncertainty, and greater decision-making efficiency throughout all project phases.
