Product introduction

The portable drill rig EP1000D is an all-in-one system for soil and groundwater sampling and site remediation. It is equipped with soil sampling, soil vapor sampling, in-situ direct push injection pump and hollow stem auger. It has strong drilling capability, compact designed for easy transportation and operation, and can be used in limited spaces or height-restricted basements.

  • Direct push soil sampling
  • Direct push soil vapor sampling
  • High frequency direct push semi-closed pipe sampling
  • Hollow auger well construction
  • Ground/stratum crushing and drilling
  • Direct pressure temporary rapid water extraction
  • Termite detection and precision control
  • Dam sampling and inspection
  • Dam sampling and inspection
