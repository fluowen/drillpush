Product introduction

EP1000W is a multi-purpose portable hydraulic drill rig for geotechnical engineering and exploration,which is a wire line coring rig with turbo diesel engines and hydraulic system as power.

The drill rig has hydraulic drive, modular design, aircraft-grade aluminum alloy made structure. The heaviest module is 170 kg,easy to assemble and disassemble,less space required. It does not need to build special roads, avoids to destroy the surrounding environment and woodland lawn, especially suitable for the completion of core drilling in remote and sensitive environments with high quality, high efficiency and low cost.
