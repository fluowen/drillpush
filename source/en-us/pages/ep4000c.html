Product introduction

The drill rig EP4000C is an all-in-one system for soil and groundwater sampling and site remediation. It integrates a hollow spiral power head, a high-frequency impact power head, and a high-speed top drive rotary power head and it is equipped with soil sampling, soil vapor sampling, in-situ direct push injection pump, hollow stem auger, wire line coring and pneumatic down-the-hole drilling.

The drill rig can be remotely controlled by remote control and can travel at high and low speeds. It adopts a walking device with a straight steel crawler and rubber blocks, which has better site adaptability and can complete environmental drilling and geological drilling tasks in various complex contaminated sites

  • Direct push soil sampling;
  • Hollow auger well construction;
  • Static pressure semi-closed pipe sampling/well construction;
  • In-situ reagent injection;
  • Direct pressure soil vapor sampling;
  • High-speed rotary coring;
  • High-frequency direct push semi-closed pipe sampling/well construction;
  • Concrete/rock crushing, drilling, coring;
  • High frequency direct push semi-closed pipe advanced sampling/well construction;
  • Membrane interface survey (MIP);
  • Direct pressure temporary rapid water extraction;
  • 0DEX synchronized with pipe drilling.
