Technologies and Services

DrillPush performs environmental and geotechnical surveys, as well as high-resolution subsurface characterization services (HRSC – High Resolution Site Characterization), with a focus on data quality, operational reliability, and field productivity.

High-Technology Services

  • MIP (Membrane Interface Probe) associated with gas chromatography and multiple detectors;
  • HPT (Hydraulic Profiling Tool) for hydrostratigraphic characterization;
  • UVOST (Ultra Violet Optical Screening Tool) for identification and differentiation of hydrocarbons by induced fluorescence;
  • GSW (Groundwater Sampling Workstation) for groundwater sample collection at critical intervals;
  • Dual Tube for soil sample collection with high quality and representativeness;
  • Reagent injection through rods (top-down or bottom-up), with pressure-activated injection probe and high-performance lateral injection.

Environmental and Geotechnical Drilling Services

DrillPush performs services focused on operational reliability, productivity, and adherence to field conditions:

  • Drilling using Hollow Stem Auger, Direct Push, percussion (SPT) and rotary with rock coring;
  • Groundwater sample collection (Grab Sample);
  • Installation of vapor wells, sub-slab systems and passive samplers;
  • Installation of monitoring wells, remediation wells and piezometers;
  • Installation of Double Casing;
  • Sampling with Shelby tube;
  • Drilling for thermal remediation;
  • Execution of dry micropiles using HSA, washing or bottom hammer.
